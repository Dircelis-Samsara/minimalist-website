Styles of Minimalism

There are several variants of minimalism that appear in modern society, let's take a look at the list of inclusions.

A visual representation of essentialism

Essentialist

Focusing on quality over quantity, the following the philosophy of "fewer, but better."

A good-enough self-reminder

Enoughist

As the term suggests, it can be described to be about having enough to feel peaceful and satisfied rather than overdone at the attempt to possess all of something.

An example in house design

Eco-minimalist

Pursuing a life of reduced impact on the planet, with a beneficial focus on the bigger environmental picture over any individual gains.

Relaxing of the spirit for the benefit of the mind

Soul Minimalist

Practicing quiet mindfulness and stillness to keep any spiritual and mental clutter to a minimum to deliver peace to the soul.
