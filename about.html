What is Minimalism?

Minimalism is essential simplicity, avoiding excessiveness with limited use of objects and materials and simple forms. The appearance of elegance with effortless design that appears anything but simple.

It involves a "less is more" style of design, with all parts exuding purpose in an uncluttered, often monochromatic environment. It's an artform of sorts about what is and not what isn't at the core, an effective means to relax the complex day-to-day of life to the point where some people would argue that there's almost an appeal to the spirit rather than the mind and to the eyes.
